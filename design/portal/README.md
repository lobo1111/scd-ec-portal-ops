# Portal design template

Generic HTML/CSS mockups for a portal: login, logout, and home (web + mobile).

**Customize for your project:** replace "Portal" in titles and headers with your app or portal name. Replace the avatar initial "P" in `home-web.html` and `home-mobile.html` if desired. Swap `../assets/echocorner.png` and `echocorner_mini.png` for your own logo assets.
